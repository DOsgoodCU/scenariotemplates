# Build your own Evacuate Scenario

The Templates are designed to be tailored by the people who might use them, 
and to be made available in the appropriate indigenous languages.

This is the form used to tailor the Evacuate Scenario.  

Please feel welcome to edit any or all of the text in the boxes to create the code for your own simulation. 

For participants who are fluid in indigenous languages, I ask you test the forms to see how well it functions with indigenous languages, and to help us understand what updates might be necessary.

The email is used to identify the code specific to you as a user. If you dont want to share your email, 
you can enter something else but we wont be able to follow up if you want your simulation activated.

Note that all of the messages that are variables determined by the participants inputs are of course not editable by you, and are in the format %%phoneresponse%%.
