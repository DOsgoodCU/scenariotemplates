# Resource Stewardship: Stewardship Champions

This simulation will be defined following the guidane of the community because it could be highly sensitive or not adequately respect participants if designed without community participation.

The concept is that it is a championship for environmental stewardship

The initial activity would proceed as below, with the actual stewards of the land participating

- Each participant would report their accomplishments in stewardship practices
- Each participant would provide their guess at how well they are doing in terms of a measureable metric (eg water quality sample)
- Participants would be scored based on how well they are able to guess their measureable metric, combined with level of stewardship practices.
